heal with an ease

A lot of people deal with injuries and have a hard time figuring out the right exercises to help them recover safely.

Standard exercise routines and misinformation can sometimes exacerbate the pain, slow recovery, or even worse, can lead to other damages when not tailored to the specific injury.

There is a need for a user- friendly mobile solution that offers injury-specific exercises based on individual pain and injury levels.

features

Injury Assessment

Users input their injury type and pain level (1-3 scale) to receive a personalized exercise plan for both diagnosed and body-part-based injuries.

Exercise Recommendations

Displays exercise GIFs relevant to the injury for proper guidance.

Progress Tracking

Monitors recovery progress with incremental updates.

Pain Level Adjustment

Users can modify their pain level over time to receive updated exercise plans.

Multiple Injury Support

Users can manage and track multiple injury records concurrently.

Account Management

Users can create accounts, log in (including Google Sign-In), and manage profiles securely.

Notifications and Reminders

Users takes notifications from their doctors about their current progress.

ExercEase Team: Helping you recover safely and effectively
